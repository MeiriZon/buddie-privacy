Buddie — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Buddie is an instant-messaging app for staying in touch with the people you choose. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. By using Buddie you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Buddie is operated by the Buddie team. For any privacy question or request, contact us at meirizon@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information — the email address, username, and display name you provide when you sign up, plus optional profile details (personal message, profile picture, status).
  • Messages and shared content — the messages, Buddicons, and any images you send are stored so they can be delivered to your conversation partners.
  • Contacts you add in the app — the users you connect with inside Buddie. We do not access your device's phone contacts.
  • Presence and activity — your online/away/busy status and typing indicators, so your contacts can see when you're available.
  • Device push token — a token used only to deliver push notifications for new messages and calls.
  • Basic technical data — information needed to operate and secure the service (e.g., connection and diagnostic data).

We do not collect your location, and we do not use your data for advertising. Buddie contains no ads.

Voice and video calls

Calls use a direct peer-to-peer (WebRTC) connection between participants. Call audio and video are not recorded or stored by us. We only relay the small setup messages needed to connect a call, and short-lived connection credentials.

How we use your information

  • To provide messaging, calls, presence, and notifications.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • To respond to your support requests.

How your data is stored and shared

Your data is processed by the infrastructure providers we use to run Buddie, acting on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and realtime delivery.
  • Cloudflare — our API, call signaling, and push relay.
  • Expo / Apple (APNs) / Google (FCM) — delivery of push notifications.

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

Data retention and deletion

We keep your account and messages until you delete them or your account. You can delete your account from within the app (Me → account settings), which removes your profile and associated data. You may also email meirizon@gmail.com to request deletion of your data.

Children

Buddie is intended for users aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

You can access or update your profile in the app, delete your account, and contact us to request access to or deletion of your personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions or requests: meirizon@gmail.com.
