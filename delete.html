Delete Your Buddie Account & Data

App: Buddie  ·  Developer: MeiriZon  ·  Last updated: 19 July 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Buddie account and the data associated with it.

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open Buddie and sign in.
  2. Go to the Me tab.
  3. Open account settings.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out.

Or request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email meirizon@gmail.com from the address on your account and ask us to delete it. We'll verify and complete the request within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your profile (username, display name, email, personal message, profile picture, status)
  • Your messages and any media you sent
  • Your contacts, groups, and contact requests
  • Your presence/activity data and device push tokens

What is kept, and for how long

We do not keep your personal data after deletion, except where required by law or to prevent abuse. Any residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on our provider's normal backup rotation (up to 30 days), after which nothing personal remains.
